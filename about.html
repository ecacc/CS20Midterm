About Eventastic

Our Story

Our story begins with a tragedy, the worst possible kind you could imagine: a disastrous dinner party. A man named Eric Kim was the host, a distinguished name in the cutthroat flower-arrangement business, and this was to be the most important dinner of his life. He invited several guests, many of which were big investors who held the power to take his business to the next level. But on this very important dinner, everything went wrong: the venue was a mess, the decor was atrocious, the food was inedible, and Eric's dreams of becoming the top flower-arranging business in the world were dashed.

After that night, Eric vowed to never allow any other special occasion go so horribly wrong and decided on his new venture: Eventastic, an all-in-one event planning service fit for the most important days of your lives.

At Eventastic, we provide only the best event planning and services to our clients. From catering to decorating to cleanup, we'll be there to help you show your best self to your guests and make sure your event goes off without a hitch.
